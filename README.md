# AS Music Theory

[AS Music Theory](https://www.asmusictheory.com) is a free, interactive website for learning music theory. It combines clear, structured lessons with interactive examples and playable instruments, so you can see, hear, and play every concept - from the fundamentals of notes, intervals, scales and chords through to more advanced ideas.

You can play along using your computer keyboard or a MIDI controller, and everything runs right in your browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox recommended).

## What this repository is for

This is the public home for AS Music Theory resources and feedback. There's no source code here - instead, you'll find:

- **Issue tracking** — found a bug, spotted a mistake in a lesson, or have an idea for a new feature? [Open an issue](../../issues) and let us know. We genuinely appreciate every report.
- **Resources** — downloadable materials and other goodies related to the site, as they become available.

## Getting in touch

The easiest way to reach us is by [opening an issue](../../issues). Whether it's a typo, a browser quirk, or a suggestion for a topic you'd love to see covered, we'd love to hear from you.

Alternatively you can use our [contact page](https://www.asmusictheory.com/contact) or email <mail@asmusictheory.com>

Happy learning, and enjoy the music! 🎹
